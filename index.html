Full-Stack · Health App

Calisnap

Personal health tracker for daily weight, calories & nutrition

Most people trying to lose weight give up because tracking feels like a second job. Calisnap removes the friction — snap a meal photo or type a quick description, and AI handles the calorie maths.

Users sign up, complete an onboarding flow, and log daily weight, meals, exercise, and water. Google Gemini estimates calories from text or photos; Recharts visualises deficit trends and weight trajectory; BMR/TDEE is calculated automatically. Free tier covers unlimited text tracking; a Pro tier (Stripe) unlocks photo-based calorie recognition. Built with Australian Privacy Principles in mind — consent timestamps, data export, and a health disclaimer included.

AI · Web App

Comic Book Generator

Turn a hero, villain & location into a full 10-panel comic

Bringing a story idea to life usually takes a team of writers and illustrators weeks. This app does it in minutes — from three inputs to a complete illustrated 10-panel comic, ready to export.

GPT-4o handles narrative and cinematic direction; a multi-provider image pipeline (Pollinations.ai → xAI Grok Aurora) renders every panel with retries and fallbacks. Results export as a single PNG via html2canvas.
